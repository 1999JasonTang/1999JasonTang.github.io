Hello,

I'm Jason, a Masters student at the University of Toronto interested in computer vision, privacy, and security. Prior to my studies, I’ve worked in data science and full stack development capacities, and have also performed research in computer vision and continual learning.

My goal is to integrate machine learning into industry applications in a privacy-conscious manner, and to increase awareness of potential exploits against unprotected artificial intelligence systems.

When I’m not busy with work, you can usually find me playing go, taking photos, or reading science fiction.
